Website Summary:

What content will the site contain?

This site will contain a grid base of t-shirts that you can buy, you can create your own t-shirt designs and sell them on this site

Where will you get all of the content for your site (ie. images & text)?

I will find a generic t-shirt, and overlay royalty-free images or designs of my own over the t-shirt's. You can also upload your own images that will be overlayed on the t-shirt

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

I believe users should care about the designs of the t-shirt, and the ease of use of the online store

Why should a site be built for this?

It will make selling clothing accessible to everyone.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I would like users to create designs and buy t-shirts

What is the goal of the site?

The goal is to have a functioning base of a website that can host listings of different t-shirt designs. Another goal is to create a tool where you can overlay an image over the t-shirt to create your own t-shirt design.
