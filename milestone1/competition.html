Competition Analysis:

Who are the main competitors?

The main competitors include CustomInk, Teespring, Redbubble, Printful, and Spreadshirt. These platforms offer similar custom t-shirt design and printing services.

What are their strengths?

Established brand recognition, large customer bases, and robust design tools.

What are their weaknesses?

High commission fees for sellers, complex interfaces for new users, and some require minimum orders. Quality consistency can be an issue.

How will this site differentiate itself?

Focus on simplicity with an intuitive design tool and a clean interface that makes it easy for anyone to start selling.
